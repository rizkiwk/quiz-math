## Ringkasan (Bahasa Indonesia)

Aplikasi ini **tidak mengumpulkan, menyimpan di luar perangkat, atau membagikan data pribadi apa pun.**

- ❌ **Tidak ada izin sistem** yang diminta (nol `uses-permission`).
- ❌ **Tidak ada akses internet** — aplikasi bekerja sepenuhnya **offline**.
- ❌ **Tidak ada iklan**, tidak ada SDK iklan, tidak ada Advertising ID.
- ❌ **Tidak ada analitik** pihak ketiga (tanpa Firebase/Google Analytics/Crashlytics).
- ❌ **Tidak ada akun/login**, tidak ada pengumpulan nama, email, lokasi, kontak, atau identitas perangkat.
- ✅ **Semua data tersimpan lokal di perangkat** (statistik latihan, skor, streak, pengaturan) menggunakan penyimpanan internal aplikasi (SQLite/DataStore). Data ini **tidak pernah dikirim ke mana pun** dan **terhapus** saat aplikasi di-uninstall atau saat kamu menekan "Reset progress".

Karena tidak ada data yang dikumpulkan atau dikirim, tidak ada data yang dapat dijual, dibagikan, atau bocor melalui jaringan.

## English

This application **does not collect, transmit off-device, or share any personal data.**

- **No system permissions** are requested (zero `uses-permission` entries).
- **No internet access** — the app runs fully **offline**.
- **No third-party SDKs** (analytics/tracking); the app does not use the Advertising ID (AD_ID).
- **No third-party analytics** (no Firebase/Google Analytics/Crashlytics).
- The app does **not** request your name, email, location, contacts, or device identifiers.
- **All data stays on your device.** Practice statistics, scores, streaks, and settings are stored only in the app's internal storage (SQLite/DataStore). This data is **never transmitted anywhere** and is **deleted** when you uninstall the app or tap "Reset progress".

### Children's privacy
This app is **directed to a general adult audience (18+)** and is **not** directed to children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no data and requests no permissions, it is compliant with COPPA and GDPR-K by design.

### Data security
No data leaves the device, so there is no transmission to secure. On-device data is protected by Android's app sandbox.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
